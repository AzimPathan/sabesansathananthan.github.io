Spell Me

For this project, we were the participation in Dialog Game Hunt

  • Year: 2018
  • Tools Used
  • Software: Unity3D - game Engine using C# script
  • Environment: From Unity Asset Store
  • Documents
    : Click the icon to Download Documents
  • Article
    : Click the icon to read related article

We participated in the Dialog GameHunt and got a chance to create an edutainment game for children. Today, children spend a lot of time playing games on their own/parent's smartphone. So we created a game which will improve the spelling of the child and took great care not to make it an ordinary app for learning words. We made the environment, game characters and the game concept feel like a real game, so that the child will feel the addiction of the game and be willing to play.

Gameplay is as follows. The player is initially a small colored sphere and will be continuously moving along a platform. The player will swipe to the left and right on the screen in order to pick up the right letters that make up the word that pops up on the top of the screen. In addition, there will be randomly placed coins, which when taken increase the players points.
The game development is in the final stages, and you can soon download it in the Dialog Game store and give it to your children. They would definitely love it!
