Arthrocure

For this project, we were the winners of hackX 2018 Innovation Challenge,Asian Finalists in IEEE SS12 2018 innovation Challenge and finalists in YGC startup challenge.

  • Year: 2018
  • Tools Used
  • Software: Unity3D - game Engine using C# script
  • Hardware: Kinect Sensor (from Xbox)
  • Documents
    : Click the icon to Download Documents

Arthritis is a disease which is mistaken by many as just a knee joint pain. It is one of the common diseases around the world which is not cared much but leads to dangerous conditions.The two important forms of Arthritis are Rheumatoid and OsteoArthritis.You can learn a lot about Arthritis in the following site(Click Here!)

So we a team of four members came up with a solution to this disease. If you have gone through the details of Arthritis, you would know that exercise is the best cure for Arthritis.But since people find it lazy and hard to do exercise, we created a game where the patient has to stand in front of a kinect sensor and play a game, in order to earn coins/points for the game and proceed to the next level, he has to move his/her arms, we have created the game in such a way that they have to move there arms or legs as required by the exercise.
